# Deal Memo

Synthesize what you have learned about an opportunity into a memo that lets the
operator decide with confidence.

## When to use

- Evaluating an investment, acquisition, or major partnership.
- A decision needs the case for and against in one place.

## Steps

1. **Summarize** the opportunity: what it is and the decision being asked.
2. **Assess** the market — size, timing, and why now.
3. **Evaluate** the team or asset, the traction, and the economics.
4. **State** the risks honestly and what would have to be true to win.
5. **Recommend** a decision, terms, and the next diligence step.

## Output

A structured memo — opportunity, market, team, economics, risks — ending in a
clear recommendation and proposed terms. The operator decides.
