# Customer Follow-up

Write a follow-up that feels personal and gives the customer an easy reason to
respond or act.

## When to use

- A conversation has gone quiet and needs a nudge.
- After a purchase, demo, or support resolution, to close the loop.

## Steps

1. **Review** the full thread and what the customer actually needs next.
2. **Pick** the one purpose: check in, unblock, upsell, or confirm.
3. **Write** a short, warm message referencing the specifics of their case.
4. **Offer** a clear next step and make saying yes effortless.
5. **Set** a reminder for a final follow-up if this one goes unanswered.

## Output

A short, personalized follow-up message with one clear next step, plus a
reminder for the final touch. Park the send for the operator's approval.
