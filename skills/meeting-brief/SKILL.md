# Meeting Brief

Assemble everything the operator needs to run a meeting well — context, goals,
and the decisions on the table — on a single page.

## When to use

- Before a call with a client, partner, investor, or candidate.
- Whenever a meeting has a decision to make and prep time is short.

## Steps

1. **State** the meeting's one goal and who is attending.
2. **Recap** the relevant history: last touch, open items, commitments made.
3. **Research** each attendee and their company for anything current.
4. **List** the decisions to reach and the questions to ask.
5. **Flag** risks or sensitive topics to handle carefully.

## Output

A one-page brief: goal, attendees, background, decisions to make, and suggested
questions. Save it to the workspace and link it from the conversation.
