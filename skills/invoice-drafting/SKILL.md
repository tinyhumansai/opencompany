# Invoice Drafting

Turn a completed engagement into a clear, correct invoice the client can pay
without a follow-up question.

## When to use

- Work has been delivered and it is time to bill.
- A recurring invoice is due on its billing date.

## Steps

1. **Confirm** the billable scope against the agreement or statement of work.
2. **Itemize** each line: description, quantity, rate, and amount.
3. **Apply** taxes, discounts, and any retainer or deposit already paid.
4. **Set** the invoice number, dates, and payment terms.
5. **Check** the math and the remittance details before it goes out.

## Output

A ready-to-send invoice with itemized lines, totals, terms, and a due date.
Park the send and any payment action for the operator's approval.
