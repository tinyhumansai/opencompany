# Landing Page

Turn a campaign brief into a conversion-focused landing page, then test it.

## When to use

- A campaign needs a dedicated destination for its traffic.
- An existing page underperforms and needs a rebuild or test.

## Steps

1. **Frame** the single goal and the one action the visitor should take.
2. **Structure** — hero, proof, offer, FAQ, CTA. One idea per section.
3. **Write** copy in the company's brand voice; lead with value.
4. **Build** the page and wire analytics + the conversion event.
5. **Test** — run an A/B on the headline or CTA; call it at significance.

## Output

A published page (behind approval), the tracked conversion event, and a short
test plan with the metric that decides the winner.
