# Firm standards

The bar every draft and review holds to.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Cite controlling authority | Rely on memory for the law |
| Define every term once | Leave ambiguous language |
| Flag risk plainly | Bury caveats in footnotes |
| Park filings for approval | Sign or file without sign-off |

Every filing needs a licensed human's approval before it goes out. Applies to
the [[Matter checklist]] and worked examples like [[Acme services agreement]].
