# Acquisition checklist

The standard steps for taking a listing to a purchase.

1. **Scout** — assess the property and its condition
2. **Analyze** — read the neighborhood and comparables
3. **Underwrite** — model returns against the [[Buy box]]
4. **Scope** — cost the rehab and plan the tenancy
5. **Approve** — send the deal to the operator to approve the purchase
