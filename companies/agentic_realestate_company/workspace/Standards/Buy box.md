# Buy box

What we buy, and what we pass on.

| Buy | Pass |
| --- | --- |
| Cash-flow positive at close | Banking only on appreciation |
| Rehab we can scope and cost | Unknown structural risk |
| Strong rental demand | Thin or declining demand |
| Clear title and terms | Tangled ownership |

Every purchase is judged against the same box, and the operator approves the
buy. Applies to the [[Acquisition checklist]] and deals like [[Maple St duplex]].
