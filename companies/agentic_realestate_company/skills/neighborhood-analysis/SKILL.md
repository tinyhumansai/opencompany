# Neighborhood Analysis

Judge the area around a property — who wants to live there, what it rents for,
and where it is heading.

## When to use

- A property is under consideration and location is the swing factor.
- Two deals differ mainly by neighborhood.

## Steps

1. **Pull** comparable rents and sales within a tight radius.
2. **Read** demand signals — jobs, schools, transit, and vacancy.
3. **Check** the risks: crime trend, flood, and any pending zoning.
4. **Assess** the trajectory: improving, stable, or declining.
5. **Summarize** what it means for rent and resale.

## Output

A neighborhood read: comps, demand, risks, and trajectory, following the
[[Buy box]]. Feeds the underwriting and the offer.
