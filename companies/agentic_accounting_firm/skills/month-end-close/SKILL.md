# Month-end Close

Take the period from raw activity to reconciled books and statements the
operator can trust.

## When to use

- The accounting period has ended.
- Before producing statements for an investor or lender.

## Steps

1. **Gather** all transactions, statements, and supporting documents.
2. **Reconcile** every account against its statement; resolve mismatches.
3. **Post** accruals, deferrals, and any adjusting entries.
4. **Review** the trial balance for anything that looks off.
5. **Produce** the P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow for the period.

## Output

Reconciled books, adjusting entries logged, and a clean set of statements —
following the [[Close checklist]]. The operator signs off before filing.
