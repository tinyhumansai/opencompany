# Bookkeeping standards

The bar every entry and statement holds to.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Reconcile to source | Trust an unmatched balance |
| Categorize consistently | Invent one-off accounts |
| Keep the audit trail | Post without support |
| Park filings for approval | File without sign-off |

Every filing and payment needs the operator's approval before it goes out.
