# Close checklist

The standard steps for a month-end close.

1. **Gather** — collect statements, transactions, and receipts
2. **Reconcile** — every account to its statement against [[Bookkeeping standards]]
3. **Adjust** — post accruals, deferrals, and corrections
4. **Statements** — produce the P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow
5. **Sign-off** — park the close package for the operator's approval

See [[Q2 close]] for the current run.
