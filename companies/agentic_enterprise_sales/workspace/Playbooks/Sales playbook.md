# Sales playbook

The standard steps for taking a lead to a signed deal.

1. **Research** — profile the account and the buying committee
2. **Outreach** — a personalized first touch with a real hook
3. **Qualify** — a discovery call against pain, process, budget, timeline
4. **Propose** — map value to priorities against [[Deal standards]]
5. **Close** — contract and follow-up; park terms for the operator's approval
