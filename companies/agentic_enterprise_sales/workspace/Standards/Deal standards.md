# Deal standards

The bar every proposal and deal holds to.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Sell to the stated problem | Pitch features they didn't ask for |
| Qualify honestly | Chase a deal that will not close |
| Quantify the value | Lead with price |
| Park terms for approval | Commit without sign-off |

Pricing and contract terms wait on the operator, who closes strategic accounts.
Applies to the [[Sales playbook]] and deals like [[Globex expansion]].
