# Discovery Call Prep

Walk into a discovery call ready to qualify — with a hypothesis to test and the
questions that get there.

## When to use

- Before a first discovery call with a prospect.
- When a deal's real needs are still unclear.

## Steps

1. **Review** the account research and form a hypothesis about the need.
2. **Frame** the call's goal — what "qualified" would look like.
3. **Write** open questions on pain, process, budget, and timeline.
4. **Anticipate** objections and prepare an honest response.
5. **Plan** the next step you will propose if it qualifies.

## Output

A call plan: hypothesis, goal, question list, and the next step to propose,
following the [[Sales playbook]]. Ready to run the call.
