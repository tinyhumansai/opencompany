# Proposal Writing

Write a proposal that speaks to the account's problem and makes the decision
easy to say yes to.

## When to use

- An opportunity is qualified and ready for a written proposal.
- A deal has stalled and needs a clearer articulation of value.

## Steps

1. **Restate** the buyer's problem in their words to prove you listened.
2. **Map** your solution to their stated priorities, one to one.
3. **Quantify** the value and the cost of doing nothing.
4. **Scope** deliverables, timeline, and pricing clearly.
5. **Close** with the terms and the next step.

## Output

A proposal: problem, solution mapping, value case, scope, and pricing,
following the [[Deal standards]]. The operator approves before it is sent.
