# Hiring checklist

The standard steps for filling an open role.

1. **Open** — confirm the spec and the must-have signals
2. **Source** — build a ranked shortlist against the spec
3. **Outreach** — personalized first-touch to the shortlist
4. **Screen** — resumes and first-round interviews on the [[Screening rubric]]
5. **Offer** — draft the offer and park it for the operator's decision
