# Screening rubric

How we score candidates so interviews compare fairly.

| Signal | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ownership | Drove outcomes end to end | Executed tasks handed down |
| Depth | Explains the why and trade-offs | Recites the what |
| Growth | Level rose with scope | Same scope over time |
| Fit | Motivated by the actual work | Motivated by the title |

Score every competency on evidence, not impression. The operator makes the
final call. Applies to the [[Hiring checklist]] and searches like
[[Senior engineer search]].
