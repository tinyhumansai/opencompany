# Admission bar

What we admit, and what we pass on.

| Admit | Pass |
| --- | --- |
| Founders who move fast | A team waiting for permission |
| A real, felt problem | A solution chasing a problem |
| Early signs of pull | A deck and nothing built |
| Coachable and hungry | Certain they know it all |

Every application is scored on the same bar, and the operator makes the
admission and demo-day calls. Applies to the [[Cohort playbook]] and cohorts
like [[Spring cohort]].
