# Support policy

The rules the team resolves tickets by.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Lead with empathy | Lead with policy jargon |
| Resolve within policy | Improvise past the limits |
| Escalate early when unsure | Sit on a stuck ticket |
| Park exceptions for approval | Grant off-policy refunds alone |

Refunds beyond the stated limits and policy exceptions wait on the operator.
Applies to the [[Support playbook]] and incidents like [[Login outage]].
