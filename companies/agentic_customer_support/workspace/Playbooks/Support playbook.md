# Support playbook

The standard steps for resolving a ticket.

1. **Triage** — restate the problem, classify intent and severity
2. **Resolve** — answer directly when the [[Support policy]] allows
3. **Escalate** — route bugs and hard cases to the right owner
4. **Refund** — assess within policy; park exceptions for approval
5. **Learn** — capture the fix as a KB article, then close and report
