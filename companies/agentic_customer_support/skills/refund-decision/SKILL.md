# Refund Decision

Weigh a refund request against the policy and the customer's history, and
prepare a decision that is fair and consistent.

## When to use

- A customer requests a refund.
- A goodwill exception is being considered.

## Steps

1. **Gather** the purchase, the reason, and the customer's history.
2. **Check** the request against the refund [[Support policy]].
3. **Weigh** any goodwill exception and its precedent.
4. **Draft** the decision and a warm explanation for the customer.
5. **Flag** anything outside policy for the operator's call.

## Output

A refund recommendation with the policy basis and a customer-facing message.
Anything outside policy waits on the operator's approval before it is issued.
