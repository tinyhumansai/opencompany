# Ticket Triage

Read a new ticket, understand what the customer actually needs, and route it so
it gets resolved fast.

## When to use

- A new support ticket arrives.
- A queue has backed up and needs sorting.

## Steps

1. **Read** the ticket and restate the customer's real problem.
2. **Classify** the intent — question, bug, billing, or account.
3. **Rate** severity by impact and how many are affected.
4. **Check** the knowledge base for an existing answer.
5. **Route** to self-resolve, escalate, bug, or refund.

## Output

A triaged ticket: intent, severity, and a routing decision, following the
[[Support playbook]]. Ready to resolve or escalate.
