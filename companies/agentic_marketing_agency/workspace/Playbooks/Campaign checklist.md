# Campaign checklist

The standard steps for taking a brief to a published campaign.

1. **Intake** — confirm goal, audience, and deadline
2. **Angle** — the strategist turns the brief into an outline
3. **Draft** — copy + design against the [[Brand voice]]
4. **Review** — park anything sensitive for the operator's approval
5. **Publish** — ship to the channel, then report back
