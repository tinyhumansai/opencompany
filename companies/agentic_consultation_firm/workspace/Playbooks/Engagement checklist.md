# Engagement checklist

The standard steps for running an engagement.

1. **Frame** — pin down the client problem and the decision to inform
2. **Research** — desk research and stakeholder interviews
3. **Analyze** — market analysis and, where needed, a financial model
4. **Synthesize** — turn findings into a recommendation
5. **Deliver** — build the deck and the plan against [[Engagement standards]]
