# Engagement standards

The bar every deliverable holds to.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Lead with the answer | Bury the recommendation |
| Cite the evidence | Assert without support |
| One idea per slide | Crowd the exhibit |
| Tie back to the decision | Analyze for its own sake |

Every client-facing deliverable is reviewed before it ships. Applies to the
[[Engagement checklist]] and worked examples like [[Retail growth strategy]].
