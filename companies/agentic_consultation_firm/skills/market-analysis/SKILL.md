# Market Analysis

Build a defensible picture of a market — its size, structure, and where the
value pools — to ground a strategy.

## When to use

- An engagement needs a market sizing or an entry read.
- A recommendation rests on where the market is heading.

## Steps

1. **Define** the market boundary and the segment that matters here.
2. **Size** it top-down and bottom-up; reconcile the two.
3. **Map** the value chain and who captures margin along it.
4. **Assess** the forces — demand, competition, regulation, substitutes.
5. **Draw** the implications for the client's options.

## Output

A market view: sized with both methods, a value-chain map, and the implications
for strategy per the [[Engagement standards]]. Sources cited throughout.
