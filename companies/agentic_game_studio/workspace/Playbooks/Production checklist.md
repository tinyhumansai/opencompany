# Production checklist

The standard steps for taking a concept to a shipped build.

1. **Concept** — pin the fantasy and the [[Design pillars]]
2. **Build** — world, narrative, gameplay, and assets
3. **Balance** — tune difficulty and progression
4. **Playtest** — test for bugs and feel; iterate on findings
5. **Ship** — market the launch and release behind approval

See [[Skyward]] for the current run.
