# Design pillars

The three things every decision defers to.

| Pillar | Means |
| --- | --- |
| Easy to learn | A new player is in flow within a minute |
| Hard to master | Depth rewards the players who chase it |
| Always fair | A loss is the player's fault, never the game's |

When two ideas conflict, the pillar wins. Every launch waits on the operator's
creative and design direction. Applies to the [[Production checklist]] and
games like [[Skyward]].
