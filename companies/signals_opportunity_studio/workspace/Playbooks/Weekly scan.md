# Weekly scan

The standard weekly loop for the studio.

1. **Gather** — pull fresh signals from every source
2. **Cluster** — group signals into named pains
3. **Rank** — score the opportunities against the [[Ranking rubric]]
4. **Research** — deep-dive the top few
5. **Brief** — deliver the ranked brief to the operator
