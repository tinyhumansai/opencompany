# Ranking rubric

How we score opportunities so the brief earns the operator's trust.

| Axis | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Evidence | Recurs across independent signals | A single anecdote |
| Size | A large, reachable market | A niche within a niche |
| Winnability | Good timing and a real wedge | Crowded and late |
| Actionability | A clear first step | No obvious way in |

Every opportunity is scored on the same axes, and the operator decides what to
fund. Applies to the [[Weekly scan]] and briefs like [[AI ops opportunities]].
