# Signal Clustering

Turn a noisy stream of signals into a short list of named pains — the raw
material for real opportunities.

## When to use

- The signal corpus has grown and needs structure.
- Before ranking opportunities, to define what they are.

## Steps

1. **Normalize** the signals into a comparable form.
2. **Group** them by the underlying problem, not the surface topic.
3. **Name** each cluster as a pain a person actually has.
4. **Weigh** each by how often and how loudly it recurs.
5. **Drop** the noise that clusters into nothing.

## Output

A set of named pain clusters, each with its supporting signals and a frequency
weight, following the [[Ranking rubric]]. Feeds opportunity ranking.
