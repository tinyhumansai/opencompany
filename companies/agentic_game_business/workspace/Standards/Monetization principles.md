# Monetization principles

How we grow revenue without spending the player base.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Sell time and delight | Sell power that breaks fairness |
| Watch retention with revenue | Chase revenue in isolation |
| Make value obvious | Hide odds or terms |
| Park pricing changes for approval | Roll out on a hunch |

Revenue that costs retention is a loss. Pricing changes wait on the operator's
approval. Applies to the [[LiveOps calendar]] and events like [[Summer festival]].
