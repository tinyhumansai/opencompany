# Monetization Experiment

Test a pricing or offer change the honest way — measuring revenue and retention
together, not one at the other's expense.

## When to use

- A pricing, bundle, or offer idea needs proof before rollout.
- Revenue per player is flat and needs a considered lever.

## Steps

1. **Frame** the hypothesis and the one change under test.
2. **Pick** the primary metric and the retention guardrail it cannot break.
3. **Split** players fairly and size the test for a real read.
4. **Run** it long enough to clear novelty effects.
5. **Read** revenue and retention together; call the winner honestly.

## Output

An experiment plan and readout: hypothesis, metric, guardrail, and the call,
following the [[Monetization principles]]. Rollout waits on approval.
