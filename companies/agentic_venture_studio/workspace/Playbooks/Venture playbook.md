# Venture playbook

The standard steps for taking an idea to a launched venture.

1. **Scout** — assess the opportunity and the pull
2. **Thesis** — frame the bet and the riskiest assumption
3. **Build** — staff, design, and ship the MVP against [[Studio standards]]
4. **Stand up** — legal and finance for the new venture
5. **Launch** — go to market, support users, then report back
