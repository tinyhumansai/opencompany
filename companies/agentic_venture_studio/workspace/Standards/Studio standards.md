# Studio standards

The bar every venture holds to.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Test the riskiest assumption first | Build the whole thing on faith |
| Ship the smallest real product | Polish before proof |
| Win one channel before the next | Spread thin across ten |
| Park capital calls for approval | Spend without sign-off |

Capital allocation and major strategic calls belong to the operator. Applies to
the [[Venture playbook]] and ventures like [[Local services marketplace]].
