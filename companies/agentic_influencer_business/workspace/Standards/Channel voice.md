# Channel voice

How the channel sounds and looks.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Open with a real hook | Bury the payoff |
| Promise what you deliver | Bait the click |
| Sound like a person | Read like an ad |
| Disclose sponsorships | Hide the partnership |

Sponsored content is always disclosed, and brand deals wait on the operator's
approval. Applies to the [[Content playbook]] and series like
[[Beginner cooking series]].
