# Content Idea

Spot what is rising and shape it into ideas that fit the channel and are worth
the team's time.

## When to use

- The content calendar needs its next batch of ideas.
- A trend is breaking and you need an angle fast.

## Steps

1. **Scan** current trends and what is over-served already.
2. **Filter** to what fits the channel's niche and voice.
3. **Angle** each idea so it promises something specific.
4. **Sketch** the hook — the first three seconds that stop the scroll.
5. **Rank** by likely reach against effort to produce.

## Output

A ranked idea slate: each with an angle and a hook, following the
[[Content playbook]]. The strongest go into production.
