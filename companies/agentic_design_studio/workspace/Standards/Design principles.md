# Design principles

The craft bar every deliverable holds to.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Design against the goal | Decorate for its own sake |
| Build one clear hierarchy | Give everything equal weight |
| Reuse from the system | Reinvent per screen |
| Park deliverables for direction | Ship without sign-off |

Every deliverable waits on the operator's creative direction sign-off. Applies
to the [[Project checklist]] and projects like [[Fintech rebrand]].
