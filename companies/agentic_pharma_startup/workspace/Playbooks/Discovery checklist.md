# Discovery checklist

The standard steps for moving a target toward the clinic.

1. **Review** — survey the literature on the target
2. **Assess** — judge druggability and risk against [[Research standards]]
3. **Discover** — propose candidate molecules
4. **Simulate** — screen the candidates in silico
5. **Plan** — draft the trial for the lead and hand the lab the work

See [[Kinase inhibitor program]] for the current run.
