# Research standards

The rigor bar every step holds to.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Cite primary sources | Trust a secondary summary |
| State the evidence strength | Present a guess as fact |
| Record what failed | Bury negative results |
| Leave lab work to humans | Claim wet-lab results |

The agents do research and design; a human runs the laboratory work and owns
every wet-lab claim. Applies to the [[Discovery checklist]] and programs like
[[Kinase inhibitor program]].
