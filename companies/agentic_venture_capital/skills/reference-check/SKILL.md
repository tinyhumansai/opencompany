# Reference Check

Talk to the people who have worked with a founder and get past the rehearsed
praise to the real signal.

## When to use

- A deal is deep in diligence and the team is the bet.
- A founder's story needs corroboration from others.

## Steps

1. **Choose** references beyond the founder's hand-picked list.
2. **Ask** behavioral questions about real situations, not traits.
3. **Probe** gently on weaknesses — how they show up, not whether.
4. **Corroborate** the founder's own account of key events.
5. **Weigh** the pattern across references, not any single voice.

## Output

A reference summary: the consistent signals, the concerns, and how they bear on
the [[Investment thesis]]. Feeds the memo the operator decides on.
