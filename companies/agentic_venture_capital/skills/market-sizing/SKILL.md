# Market Sizing

Judge whether a startup's market is big enough and real — sized two ways and
tested against the pitch.

## When to use

- A deal's market claim needs standing up in diligence.
- A founder's TAM looks too clean to trust.

## Steps

1. **Define** the market the company actually serves, not the adjacent dream.
2. **Size** it top-down from credible aggregates.
3. **Size** it bottom-up from customers × price × frequency.
4. **Reconcile** the two and explain the gap.
5. **Test** the growth story against real drivers.

## Output

A sized market with both methods reconciled and the growth story assessed,
following the [[Investment thesis]]. Feeds the diligence and the memo.
