# Investment process

The standard steps for taking a deal to a decision.

1. **Source** — screen the founders and the fit
2. **Evaluate** — read the deck and the story
3. **Diligence** — market, product, and references against [[Investment thesis]]
4. **Memo** — synthesize the case for and against
5. **Decide** — the operator makes the investment call
