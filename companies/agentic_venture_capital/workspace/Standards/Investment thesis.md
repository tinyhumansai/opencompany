# Investment thesis

What we look for, and what we pass on.

| Look for | Pass on |
| --- | --- |
| Founders with unfair insight | A generic team chasing a trend |
| A real wedge into a big market | A feature, not a company |
| Evidence of pull | Vanity metrics |
| Fair, clean terms | A cap table already broken |

Every deal is judged on the same basis, and the operator makes the final
investment decision. Applies to the [[Investment process]] and deals like
[[Devtools seed round]].
