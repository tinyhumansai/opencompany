# Editorial process

The standard steps for taking a tip to a published story.

1. **Scout** — assess whether there is a real story
2. **Verify** — check facts and sourcing against [[Editorial standards]]
3. **Write** — draft the piece with the illustrator
4. **Optimize** — headline, metadata, and translations
5. **Publish** — run the story and distribute it across social
