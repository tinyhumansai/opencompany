# Editorial standards

The bar every story clears before it runs.

| Do | Don't |
| --- | --- |
| Verify against primary sources | Run on a single unchecked claim |
| Write headlines the story keeps | Bait the click |
| Disclose conflicts | Hide the interest |
| Hold for editorial sign-off | Publish unreviewed |

Nothing runs until the operator's editorial standards are met and the story is
signed off. Applies to the [[Editorial process]] and stories like
[[Water rights investigation]].
